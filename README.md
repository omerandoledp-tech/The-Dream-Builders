# The-Dream-Builders

>>Player Instructions:

>Controls:

Use the upward arrow/W to move forward, left arrow/A and right arrow/D to move left and right respectively, and downward arrow/S to move backward. Use the mouse to change the camera's perspective. 

>Story line:

To appease the wrath of The Minotaur, the creature with a body of a man and head of a bull, kids used to be sacrificed.
You are one of those kids sacrificed to The Minotaur.
All other kids are dead, eaten alive by this vicious creature. You are the only one alive right now.
You saw a chance when The Minotaur was distracted and managed to runaway from him.
You are currently trapped in the maze made to keep the Minotaur away from the outside world.
To get out from this maze, you need to find your way around the maze where you'll find a few doors with riddles written on them.
All the best in your journey to escape through the clutches of this vicious mythical monster.

>Game Mechanics:

Solve the riddles which will teleport you to the opposite side of the door.
There are 4 checkpoints throughout the game.
You'll get two chances on each door. Get two consecutive doors incorrect and the Minotaur will catch you[GAME OVER].
Enter you answer in the space provided to you. All the answers are one-word answers.
If you run out of the given total time, that's GAME OVER for you


>Tripo AI assets used in the game:

The boy, the head of minotaur on the door and the doors were made using Tripo AI.
