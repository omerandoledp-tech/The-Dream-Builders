# The-Dream-Builders


>Controls:
Use the upward arrow/W to walk forward, left arrow/A and right arrow/D to walk left and right respectively, and downward arrow/S to walk backward. Pressing any of these keys with "left" shift key , will make the character run in respective direction. Using the mouse you can change the camera angle. Use ESC for general instructions or to restart and exit the game.

>Story line:
To appease the wrath of The Minotaur, the creature with a body of a man and head of a bull, kids used to be sacrificed.
You are one of those kids sacrificed to The Minotaur.
All other kids are dead, eaten alive by this vicious creature. You are the only one alive right now.
You saw a chance when The Minotaur was distracted and managed to run away from him.
You are currently trapped in the maze, made for The Minotaur so that he won't come outside.
To get out from this maze, you need to find your way around the maze where you'll find a few doors bound by riddles.
All the best in your journey to escape through the clutches of this vicious mythical monster.
     

>Game Mechanics: 
There are 4 levels in the game. Each level has 2 doors, you can go to the next level only by solving a riddle at any one of the 2 doors in the level. Solving the riddle correctly will teleport you to the next level.
All the answers are one-word answers.
Each door has only two attempts. After 2 incorrect attempts, the door gets locked, If you go near it, nothing will happen. If 2 doors get locked in the same level, all your ways of going to the next level get blocked, so it's GAME OVER.
If you run out of total given time, that too is GAME OVER.

>Note:
Pressing ESC will not pause the game, the time will keep running.
